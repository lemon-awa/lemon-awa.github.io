San Francisco

California sunset, Dec 21, 2023

“SAN FRANCISCO HAS ONLY ONE DRAWBACK --- TIS HARD TO LEAVE”.

I think people have to go to the San Francsico to see the sunset. Seek the sunset where the Golden Gate Bridge stands tall, beneath its afterglow, all turns to beauty's call. If possible, go together with your beloved. I always believe that silhouettes under the sun are the finest testament to love.
